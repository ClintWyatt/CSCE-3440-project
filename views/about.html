The Project

Project Outbreak was founded by Ty Rozell, Clint Wyatt, and Ryan Schick and coined by Clint on Monday July 22, 2020. The web application came to life on June 30, 2020 when the first homepage design was created. During the time of it's creation, Project Outbreak was specially cared for as it was crafted during the COVID-19 outbreak pandemic of 2020, which was first reported in Wuhan City, China in December 2019, and wasn't a global concern until March 2020. COVID-19 sparked concern from the World Health Organization (WHO) and Center of Disease Control (CDC) which caused the global shutdown of travel and many businesses. Countries around the world took different approaches to combatting the spread of Coronavirus, some were more successful than others. The United States had the worse response to the virus with economic failures, late testing, and no health requirements to help citizens and in turn, became the most infected country in the world. The issues concerning coronavirus became a more political matter, rather than a health issue, and seperated the nation on opinions.

This caused concern within the founding group of Project Outbreak and decided to dedicate their junior year project to bringing awareness to diseases and COVID-19 information. The team saw that the public is better together and informed, but also saw the need to have an engaging and fun visual representation of the spread of diseases. So, the implementation of "The Simulator" was introduced on Wednesday July 1st, 2020. This oppurtunity shifted the focus of the website to be more appealing visually, but also enhance the overall user experience to allow custom diseases to be created with the 4 most used characteristics to describe any disease; Infection Rate, Death, Threshold, and Number of Weeks of Infection. This adaptation created a whole new set of challenges as the team had, and has, never offically met in-person. This project was completely implemented remotely through using discord, github and live file editing. This project is a testimate that nothing can stop people from coming together to create and carefully construct projects for a greater good.

Thus, after 6 weeks and a lot of quality time of getting to make freinds, Project Outbreak was born.
